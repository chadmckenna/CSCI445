This is where we learn fun facts about velociraptors.

Velociraptors are killing machines. They kill more people a year than both sharks and bees. It has been reported that velociraptors were actually the cause for the extinction of the dinosaurs (though this has yet to be proven). Velociraptors are known to hunt in packs and they have a top speed of 20 m/s. This means they are approximately twice as fast as the fastest human being. Unfortunately, there is a 0% chance of surviving a Velociraptor attack.
